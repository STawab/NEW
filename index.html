My Story

My journey began with a forced departure from Afghanistan in September 2021. What started as a search for safety evolved into a mission to empower others. Through the power of the data community, I transformed my career and my life.

Today, I use my platform to bridge the gap for those left behind. Knowledge amplified by technology is one of the strongest forces for empowerment. Data4Hope equips Afghan women with the technical skills and confidence to build their own futures.
